Our History

TechCon began in 2015 as a grassroots effort to bring tech leaders, students, and entrepreneurs together in one space. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most influential technology conferences in the region, attracting thousands of attendees and top-tier speakers from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation and empower communities by providing a platform where ideas are shared, collaborations are formed, and breakthroughs are made. We believe in the transformative power of technology to drive social and economic change.
